Fuel Monitoring and Control

Keep an eye on your fuel levels to stop fuel pilferage.


Fuel expenses can make up as much as a third of all fleet operating expenses. Possessing a precise system to monitor and regulate fuel is among the primary approaches to trim down operational charges and boost effectiveness. Fuel Tank Management


Monitor the locations, timing, and quantities of fuel refills. Confirm that all acquired fuel is accurately recorded in the tank. Evaluate drivers and vehicles based on their fuel usage, and establish accurate fuel consumption benchmarks for vehicles.

Fuel Information


The GPS tracking device acquires data on fuel levels and consumption directly from the vehicle's on-board computer or through a fuel level sensor integrated into the fuel tank. This gathered data is transmitted to our platform, enabling fleet managers to easily access and utilize it.
