UBI (User Based Insurance)

A personalized auto insurance approach that factors in driving behavior and habits to determine premium rates.


This comprehensive strategy encompasses various facets aimed at enhancing our User-Based insurance solution

Optimize Underwriting


Employ quantitatively defined risk-based pricing strategies to attract lower-risk insured individuals, effectively creating a customer base with reduced risk profiles. This approach aims to entice safer drivers, fostering positive selection and contributing to a more balanced risk portfolio.

Reduce Costs and Build Customer Loyalty


Address the growing challenge of claims fraud by implementing measures to battle increasing claim costs. Enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of claims processing, streamlining the overall process. Develop cost-effective methods for collecting and managing data, creating avenues for optimized data utilization. Additionally, seek opportunities to eliminate unnecessary operating overheads, fostering a cost-efficient operational environment. These endeavors not only curb expenses but also contribute to enhancing customer loyalty through improved service delivery.

Fostering Customer Loyalty


Establish a strong bond with customers through a multifaceted approach. Seamlessly connect policyholders to the underwriting and cost calculation process, ensuring transparency and personalized pricing based on individual risk factors. Offer consistent feedback on safe and efficient operational practices, fostering a sense of partnership in risk management. Streamline claims processing to minimize turnaround time, enhancing the overall customer experience. As a further incentive, implement a rewards system that acknowledges and reinforces positive behavior patterns, cementing a loyal and satisfied customer base.
